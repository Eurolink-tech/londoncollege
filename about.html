About London College

Empowering Future Leaders

Learners to Professionals

About London College – India has been delivering corporate training and management programs for more than a decade now. We have global partnerships with learning institutions across the globe. These acquired skills bridges a wide gap which was prevalent between industry and its consumers. With these skills, many of our students are leaders and play a pivotal role in many well-known sectors.

With a track record of excellence in Supply Chain Management, Shipping Management, Logistics Management, Export & Import Management and Retail Management education spanning more than a decade, the college is now well placed to address the chronic shortage of supply chain professionals across India and Middle East Asia. With the availability of classy infrastructure, trained and efficient manpower, our job involves providing the optimum best at every level of creation. We truly believe that inspiration is the motivation behind every creation.

London College provides a mixture of faculty with a vast amount of experience in both the commercial and academic world. By integrating future demands of the industry into our course structure, we aim to provide a more fulfilling and enriching learning experience. The practical experience that our staff holds helps to develop the business and entrepreneurial skills of the students. With campuses in Maharashtra, Kerala and Tamilnadu, our courses and study materials are researched and developed by eminent academicians keeping in line with current and future industry prospects.
